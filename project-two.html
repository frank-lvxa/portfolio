POWERADE REBRAND CONCEPT

In collaboration with my good friend and classmate Charlie Cilla, I took on POWERADE for a rebranding project in GBDA 202: Digital Media Project 2. Our goal was to redefine the brand’s identity, enhancing its visual appeal and messaging to better resonate with modern athletes and active individuals.

Despite POWERADE’s solid brand identity, we noticed it struggled to compete effectively against its main rival, Gatorade. While the brand has undergone multiple rebrands, none have established a lasting visual impact or strong consumer connection. Rebranding POWERADE once more is a bold and risky move—but a necessary one. We saw an opportunity to craft a more distinctive, memorable identity, and we were ready to put our vision to the test.
